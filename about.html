Why Did You Choose This Project

I chose this project because the debate over what makes a great batsman is something that every fan has an opinion on, yet it is often misunderstood. I wanted to explore what factors—like batting average, strike rate, and survival at the crease—actually influence a player's total run production. Using data felt like the best way to cut through assumptions and look at these performance patterns objectively. This topic also feels personally relevant, as I’ve always enjoyed watching cricket with my family, and I wanted to back our conversations about who the best players really are with real numbers.
